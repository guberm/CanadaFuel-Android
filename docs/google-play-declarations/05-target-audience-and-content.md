# Target Audience And Content Declaration

Category: App content > Target audience and content

## Recommended declaration baseline
- Primary target audience: Adults (18+)
- App is not designed for children

## Rationale
The app provides fuel price tracking and ad-supported utility features intended for drivers and general adult users.

## Action checklist
1. Select adult age groups only unless you intentionally serve children.
2. Confirm app visuals, language, and ad treatment are not child-directed.
3. Keep this aligned with your ad and data safety disclosures.
