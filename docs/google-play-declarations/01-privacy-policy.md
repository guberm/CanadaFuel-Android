# Privacy Policy Declaration

Category: App content > Privacy policy

## Recommended declaration
- Privacy policy provided: Yes

## Why this is required
Google Play requires a public privacy policy for apps that handle user or device data.

## App behavior observed
- App requests location permission to determine nearest city.
- App uses notifications for gas price alerts.
- App uses AdMob banner ads.
- App stores user preferences locally.

## Action checklist
1. Publish a public privacy policy page that includes data collection, sharing, retention, and deletion details.
2. Include third-party SDK disclosures: Google Mobile Ads, geolocation, notifications.
3. Add the same URL in Play Console and app store listing.
4. Verify URL is publicly accessible without login.

## Suggested policy sections
- Data collected: approximate/precise location, app interactions, identifiers used by ads SDK.
- Purpose: city selection, price alerts, ad delivery, basic app functionality.
- Sharing: third-party SDK providers (for ads/analytics if used).
- User controls: permission revoke steps and notification settings.
- Contact: support email.
